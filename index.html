Info about The7Arts

The7Arts is an application that virtualise an entire city into VR headset, where you can enjoy and look at the different arts. Each art had a room, contain a floating bar with some helpful information about that art, and some free examples. If you need the premium examples, don't worry, we got you there. You can make an account, then buy a ticket from our store online to do multiple premium things. First of all, you download our application in your device, then you connect it into a VR Headset (Preferable if it was a Meta Oculus Headset). Once you complete all these things, you'll get into our login page, wich is in this website, if you want to connect, do it, if you dont, just skip we don't mind. Secondly, you will find yourself in a room that contain 8 door, 7 of them are the 7 Arts, and one of them is an easter egg.
